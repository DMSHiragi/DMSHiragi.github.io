My name is Matthew Armstrong

I'm a third year in SFU's SIAT program, and a filmmaker who wishes to bring my own experiences as a post-war Japanese canadian to the screen. I have experience in Adobe Photoshop, Aftereffects, and Premier, all of which are skills I have used in various film competitions. I wish to use film as a collaborative storytelling medium to spread meaningful messages not only about my own experiences, but about the experiences and voices of cultural groups outside of my own. By bringing various personal experiences to a single project, I believe that it is possible to create a meaningful film experience for the widest audience.
